Limitations

I may think that I am good, but not good enough to the point of becoming a perfect individual. This is because I strongly believe that there is no one out there who is perfect. We are all humans and avoiding imperfection is impossible in life. We all have limitations, so here are mine >>>

  • Focus

    I tend to lose focus very quickly especially during classes and toward things that I don't really fancy at. According to the latest research done by various Universities, I really think that I have a shorter attention span than a goldfish!

  • Time Management

    Procrastination is what I am really good at. Not that I only like to do my work last minute, I find that I can only get the motivaation to do so last minute. Being not able to manage time efficiently often causes me to be late for appointments as I practice the "Malaysian Timing" which is not a good habit.

  • Uncertainty

    I always worry too much over issues that are not even important and I tend to overthink a lot. I am afraid to make decisions, even small ones. I don't even know which route to take. Which certification or sector should I pursue? Which degree should I pursue? It really bothers me.

  • Physical

    Do you have some physical limitation that is holding you back from getting your dream job? An example of me is I wanted to be an airline pilot since young. But I have a high level of astigmatism, which is the main factor of pulling me back. Even if I insist to be a pilot, I may become successful, but I will put the life of others at risk.
