What is Alpha Travel?

I am a student from Taylor's University. Currently pursuing my studies here with Taylor's. I am enrolled to the Computing Foundation Program and I spend most of the time completing various tasks given with not much time left for myself.

From road trips to dessert hunting, I like to do all sorts of fun things when there's no class. I always ensure that I'm cheerful, strong and positive. I may not be physically strong but on the inside I definitely am!

Personally, travelling is like a part of me, I simply can't immagine myself living in a life where I don't travel. So, this website will be a platform for me to show my travel reviews and give out tips and tricks. Besides, I'll also share what I experience and also contents of my travelling journey.
